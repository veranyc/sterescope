New York Public Library

I hope that you have enjoyed the stereoscope photos I have shared. These are just a small handful of the over 72,000 photos in the Robert N. Dennis collection housed at the New York Public Library.

Mr. and Mrs. Dennis collected these photos over sixty years and gifted the NYPL the collection in two presentations during the 1940s and 1980s. It is through their generous gift that we may all be able to enjoy these stereoscope photos.

To learn more about the collection and browse through it yourself, please start by checking out these resources by clicking on these links.

(Note: The Stereographanimator is a fun online tool which allows you to create and share 3D animated GIF images based on stereographs)
